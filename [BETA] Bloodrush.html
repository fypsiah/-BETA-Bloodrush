How to Play
🎮
WASD / Arrow Keys — Move your character
⚡
Hold SHIFT — Sprint (faster, but takes damage!)
💀
The faster you move, the more damage you take per second. Standing still = safe. Sprinting = death.
👾
Enemies chase you. Touching them deals burst damage. Kill them by touching them while sprinting.
💊
Collect golden pickups for health. Standing still slowly regenerates HP.
📈
Waves get harder. Your score climbs with time. Survive as long as possible.
